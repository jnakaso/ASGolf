Rules

Scheduling

  • It is assumed that everyone is playing unless you notify the tournament chairman 2 weeks prior to the tournament that you cannot play.
  • If there are last minute cancellations, it will be at the golf course's discretion if you will be charged for your green fees or not.

Scoring

  • Members are required to play three (3) rounds before qualifying for tournament points and earnings.
  • New members will start in B-Flight for KP competition until qualified.
  • Each flight will award points and prize money for 1st, 2nd, and 3rd place finishes for medal play.
  • The points and prize money for 1st, 2nd, and 3rd place finishes are 9, 6, 3 points and $40, $25, $15 respectively.
  • The points and prize money are split in the event of ties.
  • Each flight will award $3 for each par 3 for a tournament.
  • Where courses have more than four par 3 holes, the tournament chairman will select the holes that will be used for KP competition.

Handicaps

  • Handicaps are calculated using the best five of the last ten tournament scores
  • A member's handicap will be, if there they have less than three tournament scores.
  • For members with between 2 and 10 scores, the handicap will be based on the best half, rounding up, of existing scores.
  • Handicap index = 0.96 * average(course slope * (adjusted score - course rating) / course slope)
  • The club has agreed to a maximum of 40 on the index.
  • The handicap for a tournament will be multiplied by the course slope.
  • The handicap chairman must be supplied with beer while entering scores for each tournament.
